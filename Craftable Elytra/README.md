# Craftable Elytra

> Creates an elytra crafting recipe.

</div>

The recipe requires:

-   3 **S**hulker **S**hells
-   2 **P**hantom **M**embranes
-   2 **P**urpur **B**locks
-   1 **Di**amond
-   1 **E**ye of **E**nder

In the order:

-   `PB` `SS` `PB`
-   `SS` `Di` `SS`
-   `PM` `EE` `PM`
